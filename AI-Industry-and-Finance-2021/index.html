At its events, the TFV Networking Event Series Artificial Intelligence brings together different industries, disciplines and competencies and stimulates an active exchange among the participants. As a series, it creates the continuity necessary for new teams and collaborative projects to emerge.

Event Series Structure

Networking Event Series – Artificial Intelligence – AI Conferences

Decription

• Held annually in September, Duration: ½ day
• Four thematic blocks: AI in Finance, AI in Industry, Regulatory Technology in Finance, Ethical Questions in AI
• Practical and theoretical foundations are laid
• New research and new product ideas are presented
• Cooperations and first talks are started
• Methods for technology and knowledge transfer: match-making for participants, exchange of knowledge via lectures and panel discussions; Concrete results of innovation projects are presented
• Innosuisse: Four success stories each from innovation projects and two in-depth methods of best practices

Networking Event Series – Artificial Intelligence – Innovation Workshops

Decription

• Held annually in March, Duration: ½ day
• Topics of the September conferences will be dealt with in specific teams/workshops from industry and science
• Exchange of new innovative products
• Start of new innovation cooperations
• Methods for technology and knowledge transfer: match-making for participants, an integrated workshop on Innosuisse, and general innovation promotion
• Creation of ideas via brainstorming sessions and modern methods
• Presentation of best practice procedures and introducing use cases that have achieved innovation in artificial intelligence
• Innosuisse: Two success stories each from innovation projects and four in-depth methods on best practices

Networking Event Series – Artificial Intelligence – Collaboration Workshops

Decription

• Held once a year in May, Duration 1h - ½ day
• Smaller workshops together with partner universities
• Preparatory work on the topics for the conference
• Aim for technology and knowledge transfer
• Creation of ideas via brainstorming sessions and modern methods
